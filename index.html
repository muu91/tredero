FAQ

Help Center

  • Which account is best for trading?

    All Tredero are great for trading but each account has its own unique features and is tailored to the experience and needs of different traders. If you are a beginner, it would be ideal to start your trading journey by using an Entry account.

  • How can I open a trading account?

    Opening an account with Tredero is easy, fast and accessible. All you have to do is follow these simple steps:

    • – Click on the button, making the choice between account types.
    • – Go through the registration process, providing the necessary information.
    • – Once the registration is completed, verify your account and start trading.

    What does the verification process entail and what documentation do I need to provide? Opening an Account with Tredero requires the following documents as part of the identity verification process:

    Proof of Identity

    A clear, colored copy of either a valid government-issued identification document such as:

    • – Passport
    • – Driver’s license
    • – ID card

    Please ensure that Your name, Date of birth and Picture are clearly visible on the copy

    Proof of Residency

    In order to verify your place of residence, you will need to provide one of the following:

    • – A utility bill, such as water, electricity, gas, local authority waste disposal or land-line phone
    • – Bank Statement
    • – Tax Bill

    Please make sure that copy of your Proof of Residency contains Your name, Address and Date of issue (the document must be issued within the last six months)

    Please note that the information on your documentation must match the information you have used to register your account.

  • Are there any requirements for the Tredero withdrawal process?

    In order to be eligible to withdraw your money from your account it is critical to note that your account must be verified. Verifying your identity and account is a requirement by the regulatory body and a core component of the Know Your Customer (KYC) protocol.

  • How long does it take to withdraw funds from my Tredero account?

    We understand that speed of service is one of the most important things in modern day business which is exactly why our withdrawal process only takes 1-2 working days. It is important to note that Tredero operates from Monday to Friday, excluding weekends and other public holidays or festive days included in the official calendar, something that might affect the processing of your withdrawal requests. Another thing we should make clear is that Tredero is responsible for only one part of the withdrawal with banking institutions and electronic payment systems being responsible for the rest. This means that delays can be a result of any number of possible reasons. You can rest assured that we do our best to receive your funds as fast as possible.

  • How is Tredero’s withdrawal process different to other brokers?

    At Tredero, we take great pride in ensuring the safety of your funds and the speed of withdrawal execution. Our team is doing everything and anything in its power to create a simple, easy and seamless withdrawal process with as few hurdles as possible.

  • What withdrawal methods are available at Tredero?

    Tredero follows the strict regulatory requirements outlined by the industry. As such, withdrawals are only available using the same payment methods used to fund your account. It is very important to note that you are allowed to withdraw up to 100% of your deposit to your credit or debit card. The rest can be withdrawn using different methods outlined by you, using your Tredero account. Let’s use an example to give you some context. If you make a credit card deposit of $2,000 and you made a $2,500 profit, the first $2,000 you choose to withdraw will have to be withdrawn back to the original credit card used to make the deposit. The rest of the $2,500, can be withdrawn using a method of your choice such as wire transfer, et.

  • What deposit methods are made available by Tredero?

    Tredero understands the need for convenience when depositing funds to your account which is exactly why we offer a wide array of deposit methods, including:

    • – Credit Cards
    • – Wire Transfer
    • – e-Payments such as Skrill, WebMoney and Neteller etc.

    How long does it take to deposit funds with Tredero?

    Credit Cards and Debit Cards – If you choose to use a credit card to make a deposit, you should expect the deposit to happen almost instantly. For any delays, please contact the Tredero customer support and our team will look into the issue for you.

    e-Payments – e-Payments methods include Neteller, Skrill, WebMoney and others. E-Wallet deposits are instant.

    Wire Transfer – Wire transfer deposits can take up to 7 business days to be executed, depending on the preferred banking institutions and locale. The reason for the prolonged time period is owed to the banking system protocols and verification processes. In order to make the wire transfer process faster and easier, please provide Tredero with the swift code or the transfer receipt so that we may track the deposit.

  • What does the Tredero deposit process involve?

    Depositing money to your Tredero account is simple, easy and intuitive. Simply log into your Tredero account, click ‘Deposit’ and choose your preferred deposit method. Once you do that, follow the steps outlined and you will have money in your account in no time.

  • What happens when the credit card I used to deposit funds expires?

    If your credit card expires, simply draft a quick email explaining the situation and send it through to a Tredero Customer Support specialist. The specialist will then guide you through the steps on how to update credit card details on your account.
